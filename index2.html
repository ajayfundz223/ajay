ABOUT US

Founded in 1974, Wecon Limited started to participate in construction market in 1984. In response to the business needs and future development, Wecon Construction & Engineering Limited was established in 1990 to undertake the building construction in private sector.

Wecon is on the List of Approved Contractors (Group C probation) for public works in the category of Buildings and a registered General Building Contractor. It is also one of the list Building Contractor in Building (New Works) / Group NW 1 and Building (Maintenance)/Group M2 of Housing Authority. For the past decades years, Wecon have involved and completed varieties of projects, range from commercial, residential, institutional, public, NGO and from private sector including foundation and superstructure, either composed of conventional reinforced concrete construction or with precast concrete façade, wall or external stone cladding, etc.

Wecon have established a long term relationship with a variety of suppliers, plant providers, subcontractors, specialists and financial institutions that we can integrate and interlink them to deliver best quality, timely, high technological, responsive and valuable products and services to our clients.
